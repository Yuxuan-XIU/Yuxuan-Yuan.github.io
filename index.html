My name is Yuxuan Yuan!

I am currently a third-year student at the Department of Astronomy, School of Physical Sciences, University of Science and Technology of China.

I am an enthusiast for stars and science fiction, which has guided me onto the path of astronomy.

My main research interests are exoplanet atmospheric observations, characterization, and AI for exoplanets.

If you would like to discuss interesting topics, feel free to reach out to me!

My hometown

I was born in a small rural village in southern China. Thanks to this, I was able to enjoy the beautiful starry sky during my childhood. At the same time, I loved looking at the rice fields, blue sky, and white clouds of my hometown, which always brought a sense of peace and beauty to my heart.

Coming from a rural education background, I want to do something for my hometown and all the children in rural areas. Recently, I have been planning a public welfare lecture for rural students. If you are interested, please contact me!
